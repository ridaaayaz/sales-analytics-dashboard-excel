# 📊 Sales Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **Sales Analytics Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze retail sales performance and support data-driven business decisions. The dashboard combines **Power Query**, **Pivot Tables**, **Pivot Charts**, and **Slicers** to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights.

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project demonstrates the complete Excel analytics workflow—from data cleaning and transformation to interactive dashboard creation. Users can explore sales performance, profitability, regional trends, customer segments, and product performance through dynamic visualizations and filters.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective was to develop a centralized dashboard that enables management to:

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyze monthly sales and profit trends
- Compare regional and category performance
- Identify high-performing products
- Support data-driven decision-making

## 🧹 Data Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and transformed using **Power Query** by:

- Removing duplicate records
- Removing blank rows
- Correcting data types
- Trimming unnecessary spaces
- Cleaning hidden characters
- Creating calculated columns:
  - Order Year
  - Order Month
  - Quarter
  - Month Number

## 💡 Key Insights

- 📈 Technology generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
- 🌎 The West region recorded the strongest overall sales performance.
- 👥 Consumer customers contributed the largest share of total revenue.
- 📅 Sales and profit peaked during the fourth quarter.
- 🏆 Canon imageCLASS 2200 was the highest-selling product.
